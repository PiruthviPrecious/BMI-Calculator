Welcome to the BMI Calculator!

BMI, or Body Mass Index, is a simple and commonly used method to estimate whether you have a healthy body weight based on your height and weight. It provides a general indication of whether you are underweight, normal weight, overweight, or obese. Please note that while BMI is a useful tool for most people, it may not be appropriate for athletes, pregnant women, the elderly, or individuals with significant muscle mass.

To use our BMI Calculator.

    Follow these steps:
  • 1.Enter your height: [centimeters].
  • 2.Enter your weight: [kilograms].
  • 3.Click the "Calculate" button.

Note:Keep in mind that BMI is a general guide and doesn't account for factors such as muscle mass or distribution of body fat. If you have concerns about your weight or overall health, it's always best to consult a healthcare professional.
